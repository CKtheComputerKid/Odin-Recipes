Sweetroll

Description

Sweetrolls are soft, sweet pastries with a strong cinnamon flavor. They're often topped with a sweet glaze or icing. The aroma of freshly baked cinnamon rolls is unmistakable and irresistible. When you take a bite, the sweetness and spice meld together in a delightful treat.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1 cup warm milk (110degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 2 eggs, room temperature
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 4 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons yeast

Filling

  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/3 cup butter, softened

Icing

  • 1 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened
  • 3 ounce cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Directions

  • Step One

    Prepare dough: Place milk, eggs, margarine, flour, salt, white sugar, and yeast in a bowl and mix until the dough begins to form.

  • Step Two

    Turn it out of the bowl and knead until the dough becomes firm. Cover with a cloth and set aside to rest for 10 minutes.

  • Step Three

    Roll the dough out on a lightly floured surface to a rectangle of 16x21-inch

  • Step Four

    Filling: Combine brown sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl. Spread softened butter over the dough, then sprinkle cinnamon-sugar mixture evenly over top.

  • Step Five

    Starting from the longer edge, roll the dough into a cylinder. Cut into 12 portions.

  • Step Six

    Place rolls in a lightly greased 9x13-inch baking pan. Cover and let rise until nearly doubled, about 30 minutes.

  • Step Seven

    In a 400 degree F (200 degree C) pre heated oven, bake until golden brown. About 15 minutes.

  • Step Eight

    While rolls are baking: Mix confectioners' sugar, butter, cream cheese, confectioners' sugar, vanilla, and salt until smooth.

  • Step Nine

    Spread icing on warm rolls before serving.
